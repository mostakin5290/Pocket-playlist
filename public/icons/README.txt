Place PNG icons here named:
- icon-192.png (192x192)
- icon-512.png (512x512)

You can generate these from your logo SVG using ImageMagick or an online converter.
Example (ImageMagick):
convert logo.svg -resize 192x192 public/icons/icon-192.png
convert logo.svg -resize 512x512 public/icons/icon-512.png
